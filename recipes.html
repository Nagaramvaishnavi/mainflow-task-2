Healthy Recipes

Quinoa Salad

This delicious quinoa salad is packed with nutrients.

  • 1 cup quinoa
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 cucumber, diced
  • 1 tomato, diced
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions: Cook quinoa in water according to package instructions. Mix all ingredients together and serve.

Green Smoothie

A healthy and refreshing green smoothie to start your day.

  • 1 banana
  • 1 cup spinach
  • 1/2 cup Greek yogurt
  • 1/2 cup almond milk
  • 1 tablespoon honey

Instructions: Blend all ingredients until smooth. Enjoy!
