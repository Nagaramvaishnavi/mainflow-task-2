Workout Routines

Beginner Routine

This routine is designed for beginners to get started with fitness.

  • Warm-Up: 5-10 minutes
  • Squats: 3 sets of 10 reps
  • Push-Ups: 3 sets of 10 reps
  • Plank: 3 sets of 30 seconds

Advanced Routine

This routine is for those who are more experienced in their fitness journey.

  • Warm-Up: 5-10 minutes
  • Deadlifts: 4 sets of 8 reps
  • Bench Press: 4 sets of 8 reps
  • Pull-Ups: 4 sets of 8 reps
